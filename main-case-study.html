Overview

Project Background

This case study was to create a mobile application for a new travel startup company. My focus was to learn about what pain points and problems people have while planning travel. More specifically, I am going to be researching how people currently plan their travel plan experiences and activities.

The Problem

Travelers do not have an effective way to find nearby experiences on the spot.

The Solution

Maplore is a travel planning mobile app that allow users to find must see and unique experiences by GPS location on demand.

Understanding My Audience

Before diving into this case study, I wanted to gain a general understanding about peopleâ€™s travel planning habits and how they go about finding experiences and/or activities. I started off with casually talking with family members, friends, and coworkers. This helped me understand some common pain points between those who travel at least once to a few times a year.

Interview Plan:

I began planning more formal interviews with potential stakeholders to understand how people discover experiences when planning travel that they consider to be unique. In order to achieve that, I gathered as much information as I could from 5 interviewees.

Key Findings:

Through my research I discovered that many people experienced the fear of missing out (also known as FOMO) on must see and local/unique experiences while at their destination.

User Insight

During user research synthesis I discovered that most users figure out what activities they will do once they are at their destination.

Therefore, I believe that when selecting activities in new places they want to make sure they are not missing out on the best experiences and that I might be able to help if there is a way to provide essential information on nearby must have and unique experiences.

I might do this by having an application that can suggest experiences that are considered must see or local and unique experiences with key information like costs, duration, best transportation, reviews, pictures, etc. Doing this will allow our product to help travelers easily find activities and experiences they would personally enjoy on a whim while also experiencing the reasons why people generally like that destination.



Problem Statement

Frequent travelers that do minimal planning fear they might miss out on finding the best experiences while at their destination. It is also difficult for travelers to find all the essential information on an activity quickly in a single place for last minute planning.
